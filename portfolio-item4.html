Examples of Business Copywriting

Completed using Markdown

In this project, myself and two other members worked closely together for a semester to create multiple sample works of business copywriting all in markdown. The first work I am showcasing is of a made-up company named Food for Clothes. We created a sample sales pitch, mission statement, company policy, and a behavior/code of conduct copywriting for this business. The second sample of work is of a userguide created for the website LinkedIn. In this we detail how to navigate the website, find potential jobs, edit your profile, and expand your network. The third sample of work is of made-up complaint to the Food for Clothes business and how the company would respond to the complaint.
